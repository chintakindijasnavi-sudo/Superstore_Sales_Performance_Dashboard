# Superstore_Sales_Performance_Dashboard

Project Overview :
This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Tableau to analyze business performance across different categories, regions, and customer segments.
The dashboard helps in understanding sales trends, profitability, and product performance, enabling better business decision-making.

Objectives :
Analyze overall sales and profit performance
Identify top-performing categories and products
Understand regional sales distribution
Track sales trends over time
Evaluate customer segment contribution

Dataset :
This project uses the Superstore Sales dataset, which contains detailed information about retail sales transactions. Each row represents an order with details like customer segment, region, product category, sales, profit, and discount. The dataset covers multiple regions and time periods, making it useful for analyzing trends, comparing performance, and identifying key business insights.

Dashboard Explanation :
- Sales by Category 
Shows sales distribution across Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.
Insight: Technology contributes the highest share of revenue.

- Sales by Sub-Category
Provides a detailed breakdown of products within each category.
Insight: Phones and Chairs are among the top-performing products.

- KPI Cards 
Sales: Total revenue generated
Profit %: Overall profitability
Return Rate: Percentage of returned orders
Insight: Business is profitable, but return rate indicates scope for improvement.

- Sales by States (Map)
Displays sales distribution across different states.
Insight: Some states generate significantly higher revenue than others.

- Sales by Segment
Shows contribution from Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.
Insight: Consumer segment contributes the highest share.

- Sales Trend
Line chart showing sales over time.
Insight: Sales show an overall increasing trend with some fluctuations.

Key Insights :
Technology is the leading category with 36% of total sales:
This indicates that products under the Technology category are in high demand and contribute the most to overall revenue, making it a key driver of business performance.

Phones and Chairs are top-performing sub-categories:
A significant portion of sales comes from these products, suggesting strong customer preference. These can be considered high-value products and may benefit from focused marketing or inventory planning.

Consumer segment contributes the highest share of 50%:
The business relies heavily on individual consumers compared to Corporate and Home Office segments. This highlights the importance of targeting consumer behavior and preferences.

Sales are concentrated in specific states :
Certain regions generate significantly higher revenue, indicating strong market presence in those areas. This insight can help in identifying high-performing regions and potential areas for expansion.

Sales show an overall upward trend over time :
The increasing trend suggests business growth, although fluctuations indicate possible seasonal effects or demand variations during certain periods.

Profit margin :
While the business is profitable, the margin is not very high, which could be due to discounts or operational costs. There is scope for improving profitability.

Return rate :
A noticeable percentage of products are being returned, which may point to issues related to product quality, customer expectations, or logistics.

low-performing products :
Some sub-categories contribute very little to overall sales, indicating opportunities to either improve their performance or reconsider their business value.
